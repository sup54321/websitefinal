Urban Renewal Strategy

The renewal and renovation work mainly includes historical blocks, old towns and villages, dilapidated factories and shanty towns. There are currently 12 urban renewal projects, such as Heart of the City, North Hill district renewal, Guangsheng village renovation and so on. With a scale of more than 5 million square meters and an investment of over 65 billion yuan in total, this project is the main driving force for the urban renewal and upgrading of Zhuhai. Industrial park development and operation It includes new industrial town, industrial park, business incubator, distinctive towns and various types of parks; the work focus is on the parks' industry orientation, planning and design, land consolidation, municipal infrastructure construction, industrial carrier construction, industry introduction and investment attraction, industrial operation service, etc. 15 projects, such as Fushan new industrial town and Hengqin financial industry base, are now under construction, which covers an area of about 160 square kilometers. WPD and Chinese Owned Enterprises has invested a total of 74 billion yuan in these projects to promote the level of urban industry development in all respects, and contribute to industrial upgrading and cluster development.
